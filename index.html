Tokenomics

Total Supply: 1,000 $ONEK tokens, ensuring unparalleled scarcity and potential for value appreciation.

Distribution: 100% fair launch with no pre-mined tokens. All tokens are dedicated to liquidity pools and community-driven initiatives.

Utility: $ONEK holders gain access to exclusive governance rights, premium community events, and potential partner airdrops.

Deflationary Mechanism: A 1% transaction fee is burned on each trade, gradually reducing the supply and rewarding long-term holders.

Security: Fully audited smart contract with ownership renounced, ensuring transparency and trust.
